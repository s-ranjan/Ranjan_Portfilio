About Me

I am an MCA student at St. Philomena College (Autonomous), Puttur, with a deep interest in Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. My academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation in both theoretical concepts and practical implementation across data-driven technologies.

I enjoy working with data — from cleaning and exploring datasets to building predictive models and visualizing insights. I have hands-on experience with Python libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, and a growing understanding of machine learning frameworks and algorithms including regression, classification, and neural networks.

Beyond academics, I have developed a full-stack e-commerce web application (StepZone) using PHP, MySQL, and JavaScript, which reflects my ability to design and deploy real-world software solutions. I hold certifications from recognized organizations including NPTEL, IBM, MongoDB, and Simplilearn, demonstrating my commitment to continuous professional development.

I am driven by curiosity, problem-solving, and the desire to build intelligent systems that create meaningful impact. I am actively expanding my expertise in deep learning and data engineering, with the goal of contributing to impactful AI-driven projects in the industry.
